First student in school history to qualify for the national FBLA conference

After taking 1st at the regional level in the testing category of Introduction to Information Technology and heading to states, I sat with over a hundred other students in the same room taking the same exact test. Finishing the exam in a fraction of the allotted time, I placed 4th overall in the state of Pennsylvania and become the first student in my school's history to qualify for the national conference.

Student Voice

Student Voice is an iOS app that allows high school students to anonymously share ideas they have about their school. Ideas can then be voted on by other students and turned into a petition which can be digitally signed and emailed to school officials.
