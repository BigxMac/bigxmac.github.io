Jared Stefanowicz

During the school day I'm an intelligent, underachieving high school student in honors classes who is a victim of a fundamentally broken education system. Outside of school I'm a self-taught computer programmer who audits college computer science classes here. My interests include computer science, design, math, economics, entrepreneurship, and politics. At my company Appitech LLC I enjoy working with clients turning their ideas into finished apps on the Apple App Store. My current project is Student Voice, an app that allows high school students to anonymously post ideas about ways their school can improve, and then create digital petitions to facilitate that change.

Winner of the PA District 11 Congressional App Challenge

As the winner of PA District 11's Congressional App Challenge I attended an event in Washington, DC where I met fellow student's from other states/districts as well as members of congress and industry.

First student in school history to qualify for the national FBLA conference

After taking 1st at the regional level in the testing category of Introduction to Information Technology and heading to states, I sat with over a hundred other students in the same room taking the same exact test. Finishing the exam in a fraction of the allottedr time, I placed 4th overall in the state of Pennsylvania and become the first student in my school's history to qualify for the national conference.
